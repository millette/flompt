How it works

Three steps. Better prompts.

1

Paste your prompt

Drop in any raw prompt you'd normally send to ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM. flompt takes it from there.

2

Edit the visual flow

AI decomposes your prompt into structured blocks. Drag, connect, and edit each piece on a flowchart canvas.

3

Recompile & export

One click compiles your blocks into an optimized, machine-ready prompt. Copy, export as TXT/JSON, or share.
